Swami Dayanand Saraswati
Founder of Arya Samaj

 

AIMS & OBJECTIVES :

  • To help the children to develop healthy minds and bodies.
  • High Character based on Sound appreciation of truth, beauty and goodness.
  • Development of the creative and artistic faculties.
  • To enable the students to discover and develop their talent.
  • To infuse the spirit of sportsmenship by preparing them to put in their best efforts for attainment of their goals in life and cheerfully appreciate the attainments.
