FEE RULES

Parents / Students can deposit the school fees before 15th of every month. After that Rs. 5/- (per day) as fine will be charged upto 20th of the month. After 20th no fees will be accepted.

School fess will not be accepted without the production of fees card. Rs. 10/- will be charged for the duplicate card if the original has been lost.

Fees counter is open from 7.30 AM to 12.30 PM in Summer and 9.00 AM to 1.00 PM in Winter.

Fees counter is open during summer vacation, autumn break and winter break.

A student whose name has been stuck off due to non-payment of fees, may be re-admitted under the discretionery powers of the Principal on payment of re-admission fee of Rs. 300/-.
